Futures basics

Futures are contracts for future delivery of an underlying at a given price. For a trader that means: fixed tick value, no spread (commission only), a centralized exchange, and expiry. The biggest difference vs. forex - your P/L is calculated in dollars precisely by ticks, not by pips × lot.

  • MNQ (Nasdaq micro) - 1 tick = 0.25 points = $0.50
  • NQ (Nasdaq mini) - 1 tick = 0.25 points = $5.00
  • MES (S&P micro) - 1 tick = 0.25 points = $1.25
  • ES (S&P mini) - 1 tick = 0.25 points = $12.50
  • MGC (Gold micro) - 1 tick = $0.10 = $1.00
