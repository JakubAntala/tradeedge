Common questions

Can I cancel any time?
Yes. There are no contracts or lock-ins. Cancel with one click from your account settings and you won't be charged again. Your access stays active until the end of the billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Your payment data is never stored on our servers - it's handled entirely by Stripe's secure infrastructure.
Is Pro worth it if I'm a complete beginner?
Absolutely. The course is built specifically for people starting from zero. You'll go from "what is a pip" to running a structured trading plan - including the prop firm prep content - all for less than a coffee per week.
How is Mentoring different from Pro?
Pro is self-paced learning. Mentoring adds direct weekly sessions where we review your actual trades together, identify your specific weak points, and build a growth plan around your schedule and goals. Spots are limited - apply to check availability.
Do you cover prop firms like FTMO or MyForexFunds?
Yes. The Forex track has a dedicated lesson covering challenge rules, max drawdown limits, daily loss caps, profit targets and scaling plans for FTMO, MyForexFunds, FundedNext, and The5ers. Mentoring also includes live challenge guidance.
Will new content be added?
Yes - Pro members get access to every new lesson, recap, and video as it's published. The roadmap includes Futures, Crypto, advanced ICT concepts, and live session recordings. There's no extra charge for new content.
