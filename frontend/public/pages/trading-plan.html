Lesson 01 - Preview

Bias on W/D/4H

Bias answers the question "where should price go next?" It comes from the weekly and daily timeframes, where we ask about ERL (External Range Liquidity) ↔ IRL (Internal Range Liquidity). Without a bias there's no point entering - the trade will be reactive and impulsive.

  • W/D timeframe - where is the weekly/daily draw on liquidity?
  • 4H structure - are candle bodies below/above key liquidity?
  • ERL ↔ IRL - is price moving from external liquidity to internal, or the other way?
