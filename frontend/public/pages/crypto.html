Crypto basics

The crypto market is always open. No opening bell, no weekend gaps - but also no institutional safety net. 80%+ of retail trades on Binance and Bybit, and spreads open up Saturday night when thin liquidity meets a whale order. Knowing when NOT to trade matters more than knowing when to.

  • Spot vs. Perpetual - spot is ownership, perp is a leveraged contract with a funding rate
  • Funding rate - every 8h, longs pay shorts (or vice versa) based on market sentiment
  • Liquidations - your perp position closes if margin is insufficient, often triggering a chain reaction
  • Weekend gotcha - Saturday 22:00 to Sunday 14:00 (NY) has the lowest liquidity, the worst entries
